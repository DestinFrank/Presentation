The career I chose: Engineering

Job and responsibilites:

There are a multitude of different engineering jobs, and a multitude of responsibilities, but I will quickly try to cover them.

To keep it short, an engineers job is to design a product for others to then refurbish and manufacture, or to do that manufacturing themselves.

Education Required

The education required is both costly and difficult to aqquire, as you need to have completed all the academic courses in high school.
What is required:

1. Advanced Functions (MHF4U)
2. Calculus and Vectors (MCV4U)
3. Chemistry (SCH4U)
4. Physics (SPH4U)
5. English (ENG4U)

A minimum grade of 70% is also required.
